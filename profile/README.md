# Don Bosco Institute of Technology, Bengaluru

Welcome to the official GitHub organization of **Don Bosco Institute of Technology (DBIT), Bengaluru**.

DBIT is an engineering and management institution established in **2001**, located on **Mysore Road, Kumbalagodu, Bengaluru, Karnataka**. The institute highlights its **36-acre green campus**, **200+ faculty members**, and **3000+ students**, with academic programs affiliated to **VTU, Belagavi**. It also features research, incubation, and innovation-focused initiatives including an **Incubation Center**, **Research Centres**, and an **AICTE Idea Lab**.  

---

## About DBIT

At DBIT, we believe in combining academic excellence with innovation, hands-on learning, and real-world problem solving. This GitHub organization is intended to serve as a collaborative space for:

- Student projects  
- Department repositories  
- Research and innovation work  
- Open-source contributions  
- Hackathon and technical event submissions  
- Academic resources and prototypes
